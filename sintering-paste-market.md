<p><ul><li>Pressure Sintering</li><li>Pressure-less Sintering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sintering paste market is primarily divided into two types: pressure sintering and pressure-less sintering. Pressure sintering involves applying external pressure during the sintering process, enhancing densification and mechanical properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, pressure-less sintering relies on thermal energy alone, allowing for more uniform heating and lower production costs, often used for ceramics and metal matrix composites. Each type addresses specific manufacturing needs based on material properties and desired outcomes.</p></p>
