<p><p>The three-phase smart meter market is divided into two main types: Direct Current (DC) meters and Alternating Current (AC) meters. DC meters are designed for measuring electrical energy in systems that operate on direct current, commonly used in renewable energy applications like solar setups. In contrast, AC meters are tailored for alternating current systems, prevalent in residential and industrial power distribution. Both types enhance energy management and efficiency, providing real-time data for monitoring and optimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
