<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental suture market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal, which is beneficial for internal tissues or in minimally invasive procedures. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made from materials that remain intact and require removal after healing, making them suitable for skin closures or long-term tissue support. Each type serves specific clinical needs in dental procedures.</p></p>
