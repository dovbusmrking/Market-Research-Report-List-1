<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Deduster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bag Filter</li><li>Elevating Dedusting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical deduster market comprises various types, including bag filters and elevating dedusters. Bag filters are designed to capture fine particles from the air, ensuring a clean environment during the manufacturing process. They utilize porous fabric to trap particulates effectively. Elevating dedusters, on the other hand, employ a mechanical system to lift and separate dust from products, enhancing powder flow and quality. Both types are essential in maintaining cleanliness and regulatory compliance in pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
