<p><p>Segmented diamond saw blades are categorized into three main types: sintered, welded, and electroplated. Sintered blades are created using a high-pressure method that combines diamond segments with a metallic bond, offering durability and effective cutting in hard materials. Welded blades feature diamond segments fused to a steel core using high heat, providing a strong connection for versatile applications. Electroplated blades have diamonds attached to a metal substrate through an electrochemical process, allowing for precise cutting with a smoother finish, ideal for delicate tasks.</p></p>
