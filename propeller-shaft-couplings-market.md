<p><p>The propeller shaft couplings market encompasses various types designed to connect and transmit torque between the engine and the propeller. Solid hub couplings offer a robust connection without movable components, ensuring durability and minimal maintenance. Split hub couplings allow for easier installation and disassembly, accommodating alignment adjustments. Tapered couplings provide a friction fit, aiding in efficient torque transmission while facilitating quick assembly and disassembly. Each type caters to specific operational requirements and preferences in marine and industrial applications.</p></p>
