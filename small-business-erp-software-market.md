<p><p>Small Business ERP software markets can be classified into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises ERP software is installed locally on a business's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based ERP solutions are hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access from anywhere, lower initial costs, and automatic updates. This flexibility makes cloud-based systems increasingly popular among small businesses seeking scalability and ease of use.</p></p>
