<p><p>The nebulizer devices market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into mist, making them effective for various respiratory conditions. Ultrasonic nebulizers use high-frequency sound waves to aerosolize medication, offering quieter operation and faster delivery. Mesh nebulizers employ a vibrating mesh diaphragm to create fine particles, ensuring efficient drug delivery while being portable and easy to clean. Each type serves specific patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
