<p><ul><li>Multipoint Systems</li><li>Endpoint Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-premise video conferencing systems can be categorized into multipoint systems and endpoint systems. Multipoint systems enable multiple participants to join a single virtual meeting, allowing for collaboration among various locations simultaneously. These systems support scalability and are ideal for larger organizations. In contrast, endpoint systems focus on individual or small group meetings, providing dedicated devices or software for each participant. They are commonly used in conference rooms or personal setups, emphasizing simplicity and user experience.</p></p>
