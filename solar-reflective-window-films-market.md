<p><p>The Solar Reflective Window Films market is primarily categorized into two types: Reflective Films and Dual Reflective Films. Reflective Films are designed to minimize solar heat gain and glare by reflecting sunlight away from the building, enhancing interior comfort and energy efficiency. Dual Reflective Films combine the benefits of both reflective and low light transmission properties, allowing for reduced glare while maintaining daylight visibility. Both types contribute to significant energy savings and improved aesthetics for residential and commercial properties.</p></p>
