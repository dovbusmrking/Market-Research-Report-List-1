<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Inkjet Printer Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printer head market is primarily categorized into two types: piezoelectric and thermal. Piezoelectric print heads utilize electric currents to create pressure and eject ink droplets, offering high precision and the ability to handle a wide range of inks. In contrast, thermal print heads heat the ink to create vapor bubbles, forcing droplets out; they are generally faster and simpler but may have limitations with certain ink types. Each type serves different industrial applications based on performance and ink compatibility.</p></p>
