<p><p>The handheld electronic refrigerant leak detector market is primarily divided into two types: infrared and semiconductor detectors. Infrared detectors utilize infrared technology to identify refrigerant leaks by measuring absorption of infrared light, providing high sensitivity and specificity. Semiconductor detectors, on the other hand, rely on semiconductor sensors that detect changes in resistance when exposed to refrigerants, offering a cost-effective solution with reasonable accuracy. Both types are essential for HVAC professionals in ensuring efficient system maintenance and environmental protection.</p></p>
