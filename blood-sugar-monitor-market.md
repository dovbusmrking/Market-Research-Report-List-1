<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood sugar monitor market includes three primary types: Glucose Oxidase, Glucose Dehydrogenase, and Other. Glucose Oxidase monitors use an enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce a measurable electrical signal, making them highly accurate for diabetes management. Glucose Dehydrogenase monitors operate similarly but utilize a different enzyme that offers enhanced stability and longer shelf life for test strips. The Other category encompasses alternative technologies and methods, catering to diverse user preferences and needs in blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
