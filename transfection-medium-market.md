<p><p>The transfection medium market is divided into two primary types: liquid transfection medium and dry powder transfection medium. Liquid transfection medium is a ready-to-use solution that facilitates the introduction of nucleic acids into cells, ensuring optimal viability and performance. In contrast, dry powder transfection medium offers stability and ease of transport, requiring reconstitution before use. Both types cater to diverse research and therapeutic applications, enabling scientists to select the most suitable format based on their experimental needs.</p></p>
