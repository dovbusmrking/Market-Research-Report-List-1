<p><p>The acrylic staple fiber market is primarily categorized into two types: natural white and solution dyed fibers. Natural white fibers are undyed and offer excellent bulk, warmth, and moisture-wicking properties, making them ideal for textiles like blankets and sweaters. In contrast, solution dyed fibers are color-treated during production, ensuring vibrant, long-lasting hues and increased resistance to fading. This characteristic makes solution dyed fibers preferable for outdoor applications where color retention and durability are crucial, such as upholstery and carpets.</p></p>
