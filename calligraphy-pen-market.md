<p><p>The calligraphy pen market can be categorized into three main types: soft tip, hard tip, and twin tip pens. Soft tip pens are ideal for beginners and create smooth, flowing lines, making them perfect for expressive styles. Hard tip pens provide precision and allow for fine detailing, suitable for professional calligraphers. Twin tip pens feature both a soft and hard tip in one, offering versatility for various techniques and styles, appealing to both novices and experienced artists alike.</p></p>
