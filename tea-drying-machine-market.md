<p><p>The tea drying machine market primarily comprises two types: tea frying dryers and tea baking dryers. Tea frying dryers utilize high-temperature frying to rapidly remove moisture, enhancing flavors and aroma while preserving color. Conversely, tea baking dryers employ a gentler heating process, using low temperatures to dry tea leaves gradually, which helps maintain essential oils and delicate flavors. Both methods play a crucial role in the tea production process, catering to different preferences and qualities of the final product.</p></p>
