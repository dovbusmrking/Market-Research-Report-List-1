<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plug-in Hybrid Drivetrain (PHE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tandem Series</li><li>Parallel Series</li><li>Tandem-parallel Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) market features three main drivetrain types: </p><p>1. **Tandem Series**: This setup uses an electric motor for propulsion, with an internal combustion engine (ICE) acting solely as a generator, providing electricity to the motor.</p><p>2. **Parallel Series**: In this configuration, both the ICE and electric motor can drive the wheels directly, allowing for greater flexibility in power distribution.</p><p>3. **Tandem-Parallel Series**: This hybrid system combines both approaches, enabling either the motor or ICE, or both together, to maximize efficiency and performance.</p></p>
