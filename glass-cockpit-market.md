<p><p>The Glass Cockpit market encompasses various display types that enhance aircraft operation and efficiency. The Primary Flight Display (PFD) provides crucial flight information like altitude and speed, ensuring pilot awareness. Multi-Function Displays (MFDs) integrate navigation, weather, and other data for streamlined decision-making. Backup Displays serve as essential redundancy, ensuring critical information is still visible in case of primary system failure. Mission Displays focus on specific operational tasks, such as tactical data for military applications, optimizing mission effectiveness.</p></p>
