<p><strong>The AIDS Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AIDS test kits market comprises three primary types: Antibody Tests, RNA (viral load) Tests, and Antibody-Antigen Tests. Antibody Tests detect antibodies produced in response to HIV infection, serving as a first-line screening tool. RNA Tests measure the presence of the virus in the blood, providing precise viral load information for diagnosing active infections. Antibody-Antigen Tests combine both components, identifying both HIV antibodies and the p24 antigen, enhancing early detection and facilitating timely treatment decisions.</p></p>
