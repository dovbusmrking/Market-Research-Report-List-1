<p><p>The Carbon Elemental Analyzer market is primarily segmented into three types: combustion analysis, spectral analysis, and others. Combustion analysis involves oxidizing samples at high temperatures to measure carbon content through gas analysis. Spectral analysis utilizes techniques like infrared or mass spectrometry to determine carbon composition based on emitted light or mass-to-charge ratios. The "others" segment encompasses alternative methods, such as thermal analysis or chemical titration, providing diverse options for accurately assessing carbon levels in various materials.</p></p>
