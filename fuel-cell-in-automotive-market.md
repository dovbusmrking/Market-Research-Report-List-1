<p><p>In the automotive market, fuel cells convert chemical energy directly into electricity, offering zero emissions. Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC) are popular for their low operating temperatures and high power density, ideal for vehicles. Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells (MCFC) operate at high temperatures, suitable for larger applications. Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC) utilize ceramic electrolytes for high efficiency, while Phosphoric Acid Fuel Cells (PAFC) offer moderate efficiency and durability. Other fuel cells include various experimental and emerging technologies.</p></p>
