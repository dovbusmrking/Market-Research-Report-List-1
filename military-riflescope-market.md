<p><p>The military riflescope market encompasses various types of optical sights tailored for precision targeting. Telescopic sights enhance long-range accuracy with magnification and crosshair reticles. Collimating optical sights allow for quick target acquisition by projecting a reticle onto a lens, enabling eyes-on-target focus. Reflex sights provide rapid aim adjustments with an illuminated reticle that appears as a dot, ideal for close-quarters engagements. Each type serves distinct operational needs, improving marksmanship and effectiveness in diverse military scenarios.</p></p>
