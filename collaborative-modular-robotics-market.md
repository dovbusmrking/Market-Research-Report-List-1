<p><ul><li>Articulated Modular Robots</li><li>Cartesian Modular Robots</li><li>Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm (SCARA) Modular Robots</li><li>Parallel Modular Robots</li><li>Collaborative Modular Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The collaborative modular robotics market comprises various types, each with distinct functionalities. Articulated modular robots feature flexible joints for complex movements. Cartesian modular robots utilize linear axes for precise positioning in 3D space. Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arms (SCARA) excel in horizontal motions, ideal for assembly tasks. Parallel modular robots offer high speed and load capacity through multiple arms. Collaborative modular robots are designed for safe human-robot interaction, enhancing efficiency in shared workspaces. Each type serves specific industrial automation needs.</p></p>
