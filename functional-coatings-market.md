<p><p>The functional coatings market encompasses various technologies used to enhance the performance and durability of surfaces. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder that is cured under heat to form a tough finish, ideal for metal surfaces. E-coat, or electrocoating, uses an electrical charge to deposit paint uniformly on metal parts, providing corrosion resistance. Plating involves depositing a layer of metal onto a substrate to improve appearance, corrosion resistance, and electrical conductivity, widely used in automotive and electronics industries.</p></p>
