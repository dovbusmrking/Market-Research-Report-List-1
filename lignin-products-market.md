<p><p>The lignin products market is primarily segmented into Kraft lignin, lignosulphonates, and other types. Kraft lignin is a byproduct of the kraft process in paper manufacturing, known for its high purity and potential applications in adhesives and bioplastics. Lignosulphonates, derived from sulfite pulping processes, are utilized in construction, agriculture, and animal feed due to their dispersing and binding properties. Other lignin products encompass a variety of derivatives and modifications, expanding applications in energy, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
