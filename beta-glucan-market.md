<p><p>The beta-glucan market can be divided into two primary types: soluble and insoluble. Soluble beta-glucans are derived from sources like oats and barley, known for their health benefits, such as cholesterol reduction and improved gut health. These are often used in functional foods and dietary supplements. Insoluble beta-glucans, found in fungi and certain grains, primarily contribute to dietary fiber and are utilized in food processing and animal feed. Both types play crucial roles in nutrition and health enhancement.</p></p>
