<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical acrylic sheet market comprises two main types: extruded and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced through a continuous process, resulting in thinner, lightweight panels typically used for signage and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic sheets are created by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, offering superior optical clarity and surface quality, making them ideal for applications like lenses and high-end displays. Both types serve diverse industries, balancing cost, durability, and visual aesthetics based on specific needs.</p></p>
