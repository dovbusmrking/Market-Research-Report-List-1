<p><p>The cell and tissue culture supplies market encompasses various media types essential for cellular growth and maintenance. Classical media provide essential nutrients and environmental conditions for cell proliferation. Lysogeny broths are used primarily for bacterial cultures, supporting high-density growth. Chemically defined media contain known quantities of ingredients, allowing for precise control of nutrients. Specialty media cater to specific cell types or applications, while serum-free and protein-free media eliminate animal-derived components, ensuring consistency and reducing contamination risks in research and bioproduction.</p></p>
