<p><strong>The Water-jet Ceramic Cutting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Abrasive Water-jet</li><li>Pure Water-jet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-jet ceramic cutting machine market is primarily categorized into two types: abrasive water-jet and pure water-jet. Abrasive water-jet cutting utilizes a mixture of high-pressure water and abrasive materials, such as garnet, to effectively cut through hard ceramics, achieving intricate shapes and fine details. In contrast, pure water-jet cutting employs only high-pressure water, making it suitable for softer ceramics without added abrasives. Both methods offer precision and versatility, catering to various industrial applications in the ceramics sector.</p></p>
