<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The EPE Foam Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE)</li><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPE foam bags are categorized into four types based on density. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers superior strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Middle-Density Polyethylene (MDPE) provides a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for various packaging needs. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is lightweight and flexible, commonly used for lightweight items. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) combines the properties of both HDPE and LDPE, offering enhanced stretch and puncture resistance, making it versatile for various packaging solutions.</p></p>
