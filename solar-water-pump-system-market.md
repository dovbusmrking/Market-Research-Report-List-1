<p><p>The solar water pump system market comprises three main types: surface pump systems, submersible pump systems, and others. Surface pump systems are installed above ground and draw water from open sources, making them suitable for irrigation and livestock watering. Submersible pump systems operate underwater, providing efficient water extraction for deep wells and boreholes. The "others" category includes specialized pumps for unique applications, such as solar-powered fountain pumps or hybrid systems, catering to diverse customer needs in agriculture and other sectors.</p></p>
