<p><strong>The Radar Tank Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Systems</li><li>Contact Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radar Tank Gauge market is divided into non-contact and contact systems. Non-contact systems use radar waves to measure the liquid level without physical interaction, offering high accuracy and minimal maintenance. In contrast, contact systems involve physical sensors that directly interact with the liquid, providing reliable measurements but potentially requiring more maintenance and being susceptible to fouling. Both types serve diverse industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and water management, catering to varying operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
