<p><p>The Membrane Touch Switch (MTS) market is divided into two primary types: with rubber dome and without rubber dome switches. Rubber dome switches feature a flexible rubber layer that provides tactile feedback when pressed, enhancing user experience. In contrast, switches without a rubber dome utilize a flat surface, often enabling a slimmer design and a more streamlined aesthetic, but lacking tactile feedback. Both types serve various applications across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial controls, catering to different user preferences and design requirements.</p></p>
