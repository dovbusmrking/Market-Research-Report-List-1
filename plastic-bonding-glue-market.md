<p><p>The plastic bonding glue market features two prominent types: epoxy resin and polyurethane. Epoxy resin is known for its strong adhesive properties, excellent chemical resistance, and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Polyurethane, on the other hand, offers flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for bonding materials that experience motion or stress. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, where reliable adhesion is crucial for performance and longevity.</p></p>
