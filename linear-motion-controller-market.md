<p><p>The linear motion controller market comprises various types based on the number of axes they manage. A 1-axis controller regulates motion along a single path, often used in simple tasks. A 2-axis controller allows for movement in two dimensions, suitable for applications like laser cutting. A 3-axis controller provides control in three-dimensional space, ideal for CNC machines. A 4-axis controller extends this capability, enabling rotation about an axis for more complex tasks. Other controllers may include multi-axis systems for advanced applications.</p></p>
