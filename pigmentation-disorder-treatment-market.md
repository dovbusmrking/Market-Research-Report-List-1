<p><p>The pigmentation disorder treatment market encompasses various conditions, including melasma, vitiligo, albinism, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. Melasma primarily affects women, resulting in dark patches on the face. Vitiligo causes loss of skin color in patches, while albinism leads to a complete absence of melanin. Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation occurs after skin inflammation or injury, leaving darker areas. Treatments vary from topical agents and laser therapies to phototherapy, targeting these disorders' unique characteristics and underlying causes for effective management and improvement.</p></p>
