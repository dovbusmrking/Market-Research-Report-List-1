<p><p>The rubber tester market consists of two main types: digital testers and simulation testers. Digital testers utilize advanced electronic technology to provide precise measurements of rubber properties such as hardness, tensile strength, and elasticity, offering quick and user-friendly results. In contrast, simulation testers recreate real-world conditions to assess rubber performance under various environmental and mechanical stresses, enabling comprehensive analysis of material durability and behavior. Both types are essential for quality control and research in rubber manufacturing and applications.</p></p>
