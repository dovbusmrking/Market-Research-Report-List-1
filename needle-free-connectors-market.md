<p><p>Needle-free connectors are crucial in intravenous therapy, enhancing safety and reducing infection risks. The market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **Positive Pressure** connectors maintain a constant pressure, preventing blood backflow, thus minimizing clot formation.</p><p>2. **Negative Pressure** connectors utilize a vacuum effect to help withdraw blood, often leading to easier blood sampling.</p><p>3. **Neutral Pressure** connectors balance pressure within the system, avoiding backflow while allowing for fluid administration. Each type has unique advantages suited to specific clinical needs and practices.</p></p>
